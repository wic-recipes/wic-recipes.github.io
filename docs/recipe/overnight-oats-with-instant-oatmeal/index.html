Overnight Oats With Instant Oatmeal

Prep time: 5 min, Chill time: 4 hrs
1 servings
Ingredients

*1 instant oatmeal packet (any flavor)

*1/3 cup milk of choice

Notes

For softer oats with more liquid, use 1/2 cup of milk instead of 1/3.

Yogurt is a great addition for creamier oats.

*WIC-approved ingredients

Mix-In Ideas

*peanut butter and *bananas

cinnamon with cubed *peaches

cinnamon with chopped *apples

*fresh berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)

*peanut butter and jelly

*chopped pineapple, mangos, and bananas with coconut flakes

*peanut butter with cocoa powder

Directions

  • 1. Dump the instant oatmeal packet into a small jar or bowl. Add the milk and stir until well combined.
  • 2. Add mix-ins of choice.
  • 3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. Enjoy cold for breakfast.
