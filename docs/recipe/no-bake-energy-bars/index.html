No Bake Energy Bars

5 minutes
10 servings
Ingredients

*1 cup rolled oats or quick oats (about 2 instant oat packets)

1 cup coconut flakes

1/2 cup ground flax seeds (or more oats)

1/4 cup cocoa powder

*1/2 cup peanut butter

1/3 cup honey or maple syrup

1 teaspoon vanilla

*WIC-approved ingredients

Directions

Easy Bar Version

  • 1. Place all ingredients in a medium size bowl. Stir them together.
  • 2. Press dough evenly into an 8x8 inch pan (optional: line pan with foil or parchment paper)
  • 3. Cover the pan and place it in the fridge to chill for about an hour. Store bars in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

Classic "bites" version

  • 1. Stir all ingredients in a medium size bowl.
  • 2. Cover the bowl and place it in the fridge to chill for about an hour.
  • 3. With clean hands, roll the dough into little balls about 1-inch diameter. Store bites in the refrigerator in a sealed container for up to 1 week.
